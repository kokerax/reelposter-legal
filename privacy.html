Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ReelPoster ("we", "our", "the Service"), operated by Dijital Pilot, handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you authenticate via TikTok Login Kit, we receive:

  • Your TikTok Open ID (anonymous identifier)
  • TikTok access token and refresh token (stored locally on your device)

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or TikTok profile data beyond what is necessary to post content on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your TikTok access token solely to post AI-generated photo carousels to your TikTok account when you initiate a post through ReelPoster.

3. Data Storage

All tokens are stored locally on your own device. We do not transmit your tokens to any server we operate.

4. Third-Party Services

ReelPoster integrates with:

  • TikTok – for OAuth authentication and content posting
  • fal.ai – for AI image generation (no personal data shared)

5. Data Retention

You can revoke TikTok access permissions at any time via TikTok Settings → Apps and Websites.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to revoke access at any time by removing ReelPoster from your TikTok connected apps.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries: info@dijitalpilot.com
